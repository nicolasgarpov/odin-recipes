Slow Cooker Buffalo Chicken Sandwiches

Description

This crockpot Buffalo chicken is great for hearty sandwiches that will please those who love Buffalo chicken wings. This recipe is perfect for those days spent watching football. I like to top these with blue cheese or ranch dressing.

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1 (17.5 fluid ounce) bottle Buffalo wing sauce, divided
  • ½ (1 ounce) package dry ranch salad dressing mix
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 6 hoagie rolls, split lengthwise

Steps

  1. Place chicken breasts into the slow cooker; pour in 3/4 of the wing sauce and ranch dressing mix.
  2. Cover and cook on Low for 6 to 7 hours.
  3. Shred chicken in the cooker with two forks. Stir in butter.
  4. Pile shredded chicken and sauce onto hoagie rolls. Serve with remaining Buffalo sauce.

Slow Cooker Mac and Cheese

Description

This slow cooker mac and cheese is the ultimate comfort food. It's creamy, cheesy, and perfect for any occasion. You'll love the convenience of making mac and cheese in your slow cooker!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups elbow macaroni
  • 4 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 can (12 ounces) evaporated milk
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup butter, melted
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the elbow macaroni according to the package instructions; drain.
  2. In a bowl, mix together the shredded cheddar cheese, evaporated milk, regular milk, melted butter, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Layer the cooked macaroni and cheese mixture in the slow cooker.
  4. Cover and cook on Low for 2 to 3 hours, stirring occasionally.
  5. Serve the creamy mac and cheese hot and enjoy!

Fiesta Slow Cooker Shredded Chicken Tacos

Description

These fiesta shredded chicken tacos are bursting with flavor and are perfect for a festive gathering. Let your slow cooker do the work, and you'll have delicious and tender chicken ready to be turned into flavorful tacos!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1 jar (16 ounces) salsa
  • 1 can (15 ounces) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup frozen corn kernels
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced

Steps

  1. Place the chicken breasts in the slow cooker and sprinkle the taco seasoning over them.
  2. Pour the salsa, black beans, corn, and diced bell peppers over the chicken.
  3. Cover and cook on Low for 6 to 7 hours or until the chicken is tender and easily shreds.
  4. Use two forks to shred the chicken in the slow cooker.
  5. Serve the shredded chicken in warm tortillas, and top with your favorite taco toppings.
